# Changelog

## v2.0.0 – 2026-01-29
- Major update to the Vulnerability Management Maturity Model (VMMM)
- Reorganized into lifecycle phases: Prepare, Identify, Analyze, Communicate, and Treat
- Expanded domain coverage with new capabilities including:
  - Data Quality & Source of Truth
  - Third-Party VM Readiness
  - Application & Service Discovery
  - Vulnerability Disclosure Management
- Enhanced domain descriptions and maturity criteria
- Refined classification system (Foundational, Enhanced, Strategic)

## v1.0.0 – [Prior Release]
